About Us

MiAND Studio began with a vision to deliver in-depth training and education in art and design to young students who may be considering higher education and careers in the art and design industries. We specialize in training aspiring design majors with our team of talented and professional instructors. We desire to meet the needs of each individual student and offer a nurturing and comfortable environment. Each program has a comprehensive curriculum to allow each student’s creativity to come to life while developing skills that parallel the latest industry standards.
